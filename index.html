Healthy food & wellness products rooted in tradition

At Avira, we believe that great taste and healthy living begin with pure ingredients and traditional methods. We specialize in preparing fresh, hygienic, and authentic Idli & Dosa batter, made from carefully selected rice, premium urad dal, and purified water. Our batter is stone-ground using modern hygienic processes while preserving natural fermentation to ensure soft idlis and crispy dosas every time.

Beyond batter, Avira is proud to offer a growing range of wholesome products including traditional masalas, millet-based batters, millet dry flours, and organic oils for hair and body care. Each product is crafted with the same commitment to quality, purity, and nutrition. At Avira, we are committed to delivering pure, fresh, and high-quality food and wellness products rooted in tradition and prepared with care.

Blending tradition with modern standards, Avira strives to be a trusted partner for healthy food solutions and natural lifestyle products in every home and commercial kitchen.
