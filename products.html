1. Idli / Dosa Batter

Prepared using premium rice and urad dal, Avira Idli & Dosa Batter is hygienically ground and naturally fermented to give soft idlis and crispy dosas. No preservatives or artificial additives are used, ensuring authentic taste and consistent quality for homes, hotels, and catering services.

2. Millet Batter

Our Millet Batter is made from nutritious millets such as ragi, kambu, thinai, samai, and varagu. It is rich in fiber and minerals, easy to digest, and ideal for healthy lifestyles without compromising taste.
