About Us

At Avira, we are committed to delivering purity, taste, and wellness through our wide range of traditional and natural products. We specialize in preparing fresh and hygienic Idli & Dosa batter, made using carefully selected rice, premium urad dal, millets, and purified water. Our batters are prepared using modern hygienic grinding methods while preserving natural fermentation to ensure soft idlis and crispy, flavorful dosas.

In addition to batter, Avira offers a wholesome range of traditional masalas, millet-based batters, millet dry flours, and cold-pressed organic oils for hair and body care. Each product is made with high-quality ingredients, free from harmful chemicals and preservatives, ensuring both nutrition and safety. Our food products are made using carefully selected grains, millets, spices, and natural ingredients, processed under hygienic conditions to preserve authentic taste, aroma, and nutritional value. Our organic oils are traditionally extracted to retain their natural properties, promoting healthy hair, skin, and overall well-being.

We proudly cater to retail customers, wholesale markets, hotels, caterers, street food vendors, and bulk event orders, delivering consistent quality, hygienic packaging, and timely service.

Rooted in tradition and driven by quality, Avira aims to become a trusted household and commercial brand for healthy food solutions and natural lifestyle products.
